# RAG Complete Guide Summary

This source is a production-focused RAG guide centered on why many real-world implementations fail and what architectural patterns improve retrieval quality, speed, cost, and evaluation. It walks through the main RAG pipeline components, a five-step implementation process, common failure modes, optimization tactics, tooling choices, and success metrics. It also includes newer material on agentic RAG, vector search variants, and additions to the 2026-era tooling stack.
