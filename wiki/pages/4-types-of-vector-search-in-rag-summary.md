This source is a short overview document by Bhavishya Pandit introducing vector search in the context of Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). It explains what vector search is, why it matters for semantic retrieval, and outlines four techniques: Exact Nearest Neighbor search, Approximate Nearest Neighbor search, Semantic Search, and Sparse Vector Search. It also includes a brief future-looking section that highlights expected improvements in accuracy, scalability, multimodal support, personalization, and real-time use cases. The document is introductory rather than deeply technical and serves as a taxonomy-style primer on vector-search approaches.
