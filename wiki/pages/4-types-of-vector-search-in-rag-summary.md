# 4 Types of Vector Search in RAG Summary

This source is a short explainer on what vector search is, why it matters in retrieval-augmented generation, and how different retrieval styles trade off precision, speed, and contextual understanding. It introduces exact nearest neighbor search, approximate nearest neighbor search, semantic search, and sparse vector search, then closes with a brief view of future directions such as multimodal search and personalization. The document is practical and introductory rather than implementation-heavy.
